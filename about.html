Profile

I work across architecture-adjacent research, writing, and curation—mostly in and around Leipzig. I’m drawn to proportion, materials, and the small decisions that signal seriousness.

My work is typically quiet, collaborative, and long-horizon. I prefer projects with clear constraints and owners who care about outcomes more than statements.

Focus

  • Domestic architecture and interior culture
  • Spatial narratives, institutions, and social codes
  • Editorial support for private and cultural initiatives

Working notes

Selected work and references are shared privately, usually by introduction.
