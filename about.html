Profile

I am a Hungarian–German advisor and writer working at the intersection of architecture, interior culture, and strategic communication. My work is shaped by a Central European background and a long-standing interest in how spaces, institutions, and language structure social life.

I live and work primarily in Leipzig, with professional ties across Germany, France, Switzerland, and Austria.

Education

I studied cultural and communication sciences in Budapest, with a focus on European cultural history and institutional contexts, and completed postgraduate coursework in Paris at the École des Hautes Études, concentrating on elite communication, governance, and transnational cultural networks.

Work

Since 2020, I have worked independently as an advisor on strategic positioning, narrative development, and discreet communication. My clients include family-owned businesses, private individuals, and cultural or philanthropic organizations.

Engagements are typically long-term and advisory in nature, often connected to questions of legacy, spatial representation, and public or semi-public presence.

Focus

  • Domestic architecture and interior culture
  • Spatial signaling, proportion, and restraint
  • Editorial and strategic support for private initiatives

Working notes

Selected work and references are shared privately, usually by introduction. I value clarity, discretion, and realistic timelines.
